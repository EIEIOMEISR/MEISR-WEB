Online MEISR Tool

The MEISR has been designed to develop a profile of functional behaviors of a child from birth to 3 years of age, in home/community routines. It is to be completed by a caregiver who has observed the child often in the home, such as a parent and/or by a parent with a professional.

The Purpose

  • to help families, as members of intervention teams, assess the child’s competence in everyday situations, which might help them decide on intervention priorities
  • to help professionals ask families relevant questions about child functioning in home routines, such as when conducting a routines-based interview
  • to monitor a child’s progress

Instructions

To complete the MEISR, read each statement in each of the routines and click on one of the following: 1 = not yet, if the child does not perform the behavior described by the item, 2 = sometimes, if the child performs the behavior sometimes, and 3 = often/beyond this, if the child performs the behavior often or beyond often.
