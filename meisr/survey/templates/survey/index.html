Online MEISR Tool

The Measure of Engagement, Independence, and Social Relationships produces a profile of a child’s functioning in home and community routines. It is for children birth to three years of age. A child’s caregiver, such as a parent, completes it.

The Purpose

  • to help families, as members of intervention teams, assess the child’s competence in everyday situations, which might help them decide on intervention priorities
  • to help professionals ask families relevant questions about child functioning in home routines, such as when conducting a Routines-Based Interview
  • to monitor a child’s progress

Instructions

To complete the MEISR, read each statement in each of the routines and click on one of the following: 1 = not yet, if the child does not perform the behavior described by the item, 2 = sometimes, if the child performs the behavior sometimes, and 3 = often/beyond this, if the child performs the behavior often or the child used to do this often but has outgrown the behavior.
